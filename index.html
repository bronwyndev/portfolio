About Me

Hi there! I'm Bronwyn, a full-stack web developer based in Sydney, Australia. I'm originally from Whangārei, New Zealand, where my career in web development began at the age of eighteen. My poison of choice for the majority of my career thus far has been PHP, JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5 and MySQL, however recently I'm expanding to React, Next.JS, TypeScript and NodeJS as well, and really enjoying the flexibility. Next on my horizon would be to get my AWS Cloud Practitioner certification, and then AWS Solutions Architect certification.

Outside of work you will find me working on my various side projects, spending time with my partner and two Chow Chows, learning languages, creative writing and gaming.
